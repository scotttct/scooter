Scooter's Trail-Side Stop Cafe and Bike Shop

Scooter's is located just off the Legacy Trail here in North Venice, Florida on 1 Bikers Way, turn left as the trail crosses the road. There is also parking for 'a few' cars, and yes all are welcome! Scooter provides both a Cafe for pre-ride, walk, run beverages, vegan pastries, baked goods and light Vegan - Ovo Vegetarian sandwiches and wraps. Scooter's is also a Bike Shop where you we provide bike maintainance, sell bikes and accessories, and bike running or walking apparel and footware. Scooter's is a one Stop Trail-side Shop!

Scooter's is a place to where hanging out before, after, and during a Trail bike, run, or walk is encouraged. Once you enter you may never want to leave!
