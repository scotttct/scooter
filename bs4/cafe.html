Scooter's Cafe Coffees & Teas

Located just of the Legacy Trail. Stop by for some of our famous french pressed coffee or tea before, during or after your ride, run or walk on the trail.

Delicious Treats, Good Eats Bakery & Kitchen

Our healthy menu features nutritional snacks, baked goods, wraps and sandwiches perfect for breakfast or lunchtime. We source our ingredients from local, oragnic farms whenever possible, most of our choice are either vegetarian or vegan.

Event Nights Guest Chefs

Twice a month, or by special request, a guest chef will cook table-side healthy meals from around the world. These events are reservation only, and the proceeds will go to help lengthen and improve the Legacy Trail.
